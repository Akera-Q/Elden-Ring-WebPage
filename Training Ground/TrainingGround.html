What is ELden Ring?

Elden Ring is a action role-playing game developed by FromSoftware. It was published on February 25 in Japan by FromSoftware and internationally by Bandai Namco Entertainment. Set in the Lands Between, players control a customizable player character on a quest to repair the Elden Ring and become the new Elden Lord.
